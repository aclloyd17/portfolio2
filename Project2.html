Neighborhood Print Project

This project, completed over the Summer of 2022, was done for a realtor. She wanted a neighborhood newsletter as well as a postcard advertising her recent sale of a home in the area. I was given the photos and some of the verbage to use on the final product, but the design was left up to me, as long as it fit the tone of her business and the intended audience. I used Adobe Indesign, Illustrator, and PhotoShop to make the final products.

The neighborhood newsletter provided a challenge, as it was a lot of information to fit into a small space. I aimed to keep a cohesive color scheme and font styles throughout, while still getting the message across.

The realtor mentioned above was happy with my newsletter, so I was asked to use specific photos and verbage to create this postcard, advertising her business. By utilizing the Adobe Suite, I was able to make a final product that looked good in print.
